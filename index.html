Purchasing an Apartment Is Necessary for Marriage

Posted on 2020-10-09 | In 范文
Words count in article: 188

When people tie the knot, they usually get some necessities in different ages. Nowadays, though housing price is soaring, I still think that purchasing an apartment is necessary for marriage.

On the one hand, home is very important for every one. For my money, a house which is rented can’t be called home in our Chinese traditional concepts. What’s more, home can bring us the sense of security which most of us are searching for it in marriage. So to some extent, purchasing an apartment can fulfill our need in this aspect.

On the other hand, the economic foundation is a key element to keep a stable marriage. As we all know, people now bear many pressures. For example, many couples’ salaries can’t even afford their daily expenses, not to mention the fact that they will have their babies and have to pay some consequent expenses. Many people can’t deal with these and choose to divorce. Therefore, economic capability is also important for marriage. To sum up, purchasing an apartment is important for marriage, which can let the marriage life be happier and steadier.
